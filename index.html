Welcome To Heat & Cool

Excellence Has Been Our Hallmark Since 2004

In Heat & Cool we provide customer-focused HVAC /heating, plumbing, cooling, ventilation/ sevices which we started in 2004. Located in Wandsworth, South London, our goal is to become one of the best installers in the London Area. We are team of 4 professionals, fully qualified, licensed and insured, making us the perfect choice for fast, professional and reliable service.

The services we provide range from simple boiler installations to complicated, individually designed heating, cooling, ventilation and hot/cold water distribution systems capable of fully adapting advanced and ECO technologies / Air source heat pumps, mechanical heat recovery systems, solar panels /.

 

Our services :


  • Gas Boilers (domestic/commercial)                    
  • Central and Under Floor Heating     
  • Pressurised Hot Water Storage    
  • Pumped Systems
  • Heating Controls
  • Bathrooms
  • Solar Panels
  • Heat Recovery Systems
  • Mechanical design
  • Air Source Heat Pumps
  • Ventilation   

 

  

 

What sets us apart in the industry is the punctuality, care, knowledge, experience, skills and simplicity we bring to the job. We are proud to have an absolute ZERO level of leaks, inefficiencies and functionality failures with our installations. Our mechanical engineering background enables us to work very closely with contractors / architects / and customers helping them to transfer any ideas they have about heating/cooling systems or water distribution into reliable, efficient and most importantly - user friendly installations.

We constantly keep up-to-date with the latest developments in the HVAC sector and regularly attend courses from leading manufacturers of heating/cooling systems ( NIBE, DAIKIN, MITSUBISHI).

Our installations strictly comply with Gas Safe and Building Regulations, Water By-laws and other important installation policies such as ensuring:

  • The whole system is designed and custom fitted to the required specifications and customer needs using up-to-date methods and only WRAS approved materials;
  • Installed pipework (hidden or visible) is very NEAT, properly secured, fully insulated, visibly marked, dry and wet tested for 48 hours before connecting to the water system;
  • 2D drawings /PDF or CAD/ are provided for all mechanical services /plant room, gas, hot-cold, UFH and waste pipework runs/, 3D drawings for ventilation ducting and plant room along with a photo-documented exact layout with marked dimensions and all presented to the customer at the end of the job;
  • Issuing operating and maintenance instructions;
  • Free 2 years service and landlord Gas Safety certificates for all our installations;
  • Public Liability Insurance up to £ 10,000,000.

If you have any questions about our services or would like to discuss future possible co-operation, please contact me by phone or e-mail and I shall be very glad to answer them or arrange a meeting at your convenience to introduce myself and my team in person.
